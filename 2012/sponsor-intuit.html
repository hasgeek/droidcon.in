Intuit India

Intuit Inc.’s India headquarters, based in Bangalore, houses the company’s Development Centre and is the regional hub for in-country engineering, marketing, sales and support operations. In addition, Intuit has marketing & sales offices in New Delhi and Mumbai. India office is an integral part of Intuit’s business operations in the United States, Canada and the United Kingdom. 

At a Glance: Intuit India
Headquarters: Bangalore
Opened: 2005
Employees: 625
Size: 100,000 square feet
Operations: Global Product Development, IT, Sales, Marketing & Technology Innovation
Global Business Division Leader India: Nikhil Arora, VP & MD, India
India Development Center Leader: Vijay Anand, VP, India Development Center
Website: www.intuit.in

In India, For India, the Bangalore office is Intuit’s first venture in the Asia-Pacific region, working with individuals and small businesses to develop home-grown solutions for Indian challenges. Originally conceived as an engineering lab for Intuit’s core products, such as QuickBooks, the Centre’s role has expanded to include conceiving, developing and selling products in global emerging markets. The company has been recognized as an employer of choice in India: as 2nd best company for Rewards and Recognition, 6th Best Employer in India, among the Top 10 Best Places to Work for in India and Top 25 Best Employers in Asia.

Building Solutions

The Centre created and now supports products specifically for India.

Small Business Offerings

QuickBooks India: Furthering our commitment to helping improve their financial lives of Small and Medium Business owners, Intuit is introducing QuickBooks Online in India.  QuickBooks Online is a bookkeeping and accounting system that organizes your finances and complies with managerial accounting standards that helps business “Save Time” and “Manage their finances more efficiently” .  "Simply put it is, “Small Business Accounting Made Easy” . 

More information is available at: http://www.quickbooksonline.in/

Consumer Offerings

  • Intuit Money Manager a Web-based service that helps users save time and money by allowing them to collect all their financial information in a central, safe and secure location that provides a single view of their total financial picture, as well as insights into their saving and spending habits.  
  • We announced a partnership with ICICI Bank in 2011, to deliver Money Manager to millions of ICICI’s online banking customers.

Fasal:  A SMS service that helps farmers get the best price for their produce. Fasal provides market price transparency and matches the farmer with a buyer that is willing to buy their produce at fair price. An easy to use SMS based marketplace for farmers, Fasal offers personalized, reliable and real time wholesale market prices to farmers. More information is available at http://fasal.intuit.com/

txtWeb: Is a global platform where anyone with a mobile phone can discover and consume internet AND txtWeb-only content just by SMSing keywords (very much like entering domain names into internet browsers) to ONE national number, and receive back content. These applications are created by an open community of publishers and developers and include wikipedia content, local market prices, government programs, financial literacy tips etc. More information is available at www.txtWeb.com

Building Innovation

Innovation and collaboration play important roles in product development at Intuit and the Development Centre. As part of the company’s entrepreneurial culture, employees receive “unstructured time,” with the freedom to pursue and develop new ideas outside of their daily work. In addition, personal development opportunities allow employees to master new skills and take on greater challenges.

Supporting Communities

Intuit supports local communities through its philanthropic We Care and Give Back program. In India, the company is involved with a number of education initiatives, including the Santosh Educational and Charitable Trust, local government schools and building a new charity website for the Sri Rakum School for Blind.

About Intuit Inc. 

Intuit Inc. is a leading provider of business and financial management solutions for small and mid-sized businesses; financial institutions, including banks and credit unions; consumers and accounting professionals. Its flagship products and services, including QuickBooks®, Quicken® and TurboTax®, simplify small business management and payroll processing, personal finance, and tax preparation and filing. ProSeries® and Lacerte® are Intuit's leading tax preparation offerings for professional accountants. Intuit Financial Services helps banks and credit unions grow by offering innovative online and mobile banking solutions that make it easier for consumers and businesses to manage their money. Founded in 1983, Intuit had annual revenue of $4.15 billion in its fiscal year 2012. The company has approximately 8,000 employees with major offices in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, India and other locations. More information can be found at www.intuit.com.
