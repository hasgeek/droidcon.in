Droidcon is back! India's largest Android conference is on 2 & 3 November 2012 at the MLR Convention Centre, Whitefield, Bangalore. Join us for two days of in-depth, hands-on sessions on everything about Android.

App Demos

Watch experts show off apps theyve built & offer tips and best practices on UI/UX/IXD, memory optimization, app marketing, dealing with fragmentation and more. Learn how to build awesome apps that are in use by millions of users. Details »

Native + HTML5

The Native vs HTML5 battle is passé, learn about the best techniques, tools & libraries that bridge the best of both worlds together.

Specialized Topics

Android is everywhere and is being used in ways few of us may have imagined. Learn about sensors, robots, NFC, multi-modal apps, embedded NoSQL databases, and using special accessories. Details »

General Topics

A section for topics that are always popular, this covers everything from building your first Android app through design approaches, engineering process, marketing and integrations. Details »
