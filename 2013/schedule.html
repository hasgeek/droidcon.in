Droidcon Hackathon

29 Nov 6:30pm - 30 Nov 6:30pm

A 24 hour hackathon followed by demos and prizes

Intel will award Samsung Galaxy Tab 3 to top 5 apps that showcase Intel differentiation
Amazon will award kindle fire to the best app that integrates the Amazon API
Adaptxt will award Nexus 7 to the innovative app which uses Adaptxt APIs
And more goodies from Sony
Github will give coupons for one year's access to a private repository for top 5 teams.
