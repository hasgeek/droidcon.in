Droidcon is back! India's largest Android conference is on 28th - 30th November 2013 at the MLR Convention Centre, Whitefield, Bangalore. Join us for three days of in-depth, hands-on sessions on everything about Android.

This year, we've lined up dedicated tracks for deep dives into Systems, UX, Gaming, Business & App Demos that bring together the best speakers in the country. Of note this year, the Systems track shall span two days, and provide deep technical sessions.

Droidcon is India's largest Android developer conference, and is part of the world wide series of conferences that happens in London, Paris, Berlin, Netherlands, Tunis, Ankara and Brussels. The first edition of Droidcon India was at Bangalore in Nov 2011. The second edition in Nov 2012 was featuring General & Specialized Topics, Native + HTML5 and App Demos.
