droidconIN is India's largest Android developer conference. The 2015 edition is over. Join us next year for the sixth edition of droidconIN in November.

For more updates, follows us on Facebook and Twitter.

droidconIN 2015 was about Android breaking out of mainstream smartphones and tablets and moving into all kinds of unique devices like IoT, Wearables, Automotive, TV, etc. Read all the interesting talk submissions here.

2015 edition had workshops on Basic Android app development, Android & IoT and Material design.

droidconIN is part of the world wide series of conferences that happens in London, Paris, Berlin, Netherlands, Tunis, Ankara and Brussels. The first edition of droidconIN was at Bangalore in Nov 2011. The second edition in Nov 2012 was featuring General & Specialized Topics, Native + HTML5 and App Demos. The 2013 edition was about Systems, UX, Gaming, Business and App Demos. The 2014 edition featured dedicated tracks for deep dives into UI/UX, Data sync & versioning, App Demos and hardware.

droidconIN 2015 Diversity Grants

HasGeek believes in diversity of participation at the conferences. We would like to make every effort to include members from underrepresented groups at our conferences. We have therefore introduced a grants programme for all our upcoming conferences.

Grants are intended to provide financial assistance and/or opportunities to members of these groups and enable them to participate in the community. Actual number of grants will be awarded depending on the quality of applications. Those who receive the grant will get to watch some of best speakers from the tech community talk about their craft and cutting edge technology.

For droidconIN 2015, we have grants for women and students interested in android development. Grants for women developers is sponsored by Intel® Software and organized by droidcon global department.

Who can apply?

Grants are open to

  • Women developers
  • Students

What does this grant include ?

The grant includes a pass to attend the event and refund for travel and accommodations cost, up to a maximum of 23,000 INR. Travel and accommodation costs will be reimbursed after the conference on submission of expense receipts. Grant awardees are expected to organize their travel and accommodation.

Grants closed.

Thank you for your applications. Grants are now in evaluation.

For any queries regarding grant, write to us at grants@hasgeek.com

HasGeek

HasGeek helps people in technology meet each other, collaborate on new opportunities and advance the state of the art. We organize conferences, hacknights, workshops and geekups, and provide tools for communities to self-organize.

We’ve created conferences around the themes of front end engineering, big data and cloud computing, JavaScript, Android, location-aware services and devops. Our Job Board is used by thousands of geeks every month to find new opportunities.

HasGeek is a technology company in the media business, bringing software automation to a traditionally people-intensive operation. HasGeek has organized over two dozen conferences attended by thousands of technology professionals, made several thousand connections, and built open source tools that are now used by many communities.
