About droidconIN

droidconIN is an annual gathering of mobile developers who build for various mobile platforms, primarily Android. The conference's goal is to expose developers to the latest technological advances in the mobile ecosystem and the means to apply them when they build apps for mobile. Most of the speakers and attendees tend to be Android developers.

droidconIN happens on 10-11 November this year. The conference will bring together 500-600 Android and cross-platform developers.
