Digital Ocean

Digital Ocean

DigitalOcean is the world’s 2nd largest cloud computing platform focused on simplifying cloud infrastructure for software developers. The company is headquartered in New York City with 12 datacenters across the world, with their latest datacenter being launched in Bangalore. The new generation of startups leverage their cloud infrastructure to rapidly deploy and scale their production environments and distributed applications.

Trusted by 700,000+ developers globally who have spun up 14MM+ Droplets (virtual servers) on their infrastructure, the key features of their platform are:

  • Powerful Hardware - All cloud servers are built on powerful Hex Core machines with dedicated ECC Ram and RAID SSD storage.
  • 55 second provisioning time for servers
  • Tier-1 Bandwidth - All servers come with 1Gb/sec. network interface.
  • Simple Control Panel - The control panel focuses on usability, streamlining the complexities of traditional web hosting.
    • 1-Click Installs for popular applications
    • Team Accounts which allow multiple users to share resources without having to share login or billing information
    • Auto Backups and Snapshots
  • Simple API – A simple name spaced API that provides complete control over your virtual private servers
  • Digitalocean community tutorials is the #1 resource for developers when they are looking for new technical tutorials for new technology (not just related to DigitalOcean)
  • Free 24/7 access to their customer support team

Udacity

Udacity was born out of a Stanford University experiment in which Sebastian Thrun and Peter Norvig offered their "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence" course online to anyone, for free. Over 160,000 students in more than 190 countries enrolled and not much later, Udacity was born.

Udacity is building an online university, "by Silicon Valley", that:

  • teaches the skills that industry employers need today
  • delivers credentials endorsed by employers
  • educates at a fraction of the cost of traditional schools

With industry giants—Google, AT&T, Facebook, Salesforce, Cloudera, etc.—we offer Nanodegree programs and credentials, designed so professionals become Web Developers, Data Analysts, Mobile Developers, etc. Our students acquire real skills through a series of online courses and hands-on projects.

Our mission is to bring accessible, affordable, engaging, and highly effective higher education to the world.
